Stylized Low-Poly Art Style Guide v0.1

🧱 Geometry

    * Silhouette Style: Graphically accurate and expressive

    * Edge Design: Varied edge geometry with intentional asymmetry

    * Beveling: Allowed where it enhances shape recognition or lighting response

🎨 Color Palette

    * Dusty Red-Orange — #C24A2D (terrain, accents, danger)

    * Ghostly Green — #9AD8AE (spirit themes, spectral lighting)

    * Deep Purple — #4B2C5E (twilight sky, magical elements)

🖌️ Textures

    * Base Style: Flat color fills

    * Detail: Light gradient ramps to create depth and contrast

    * Avoid: PBR/realistic textures — stylization over realism

💡 Lighting

    * Primary Light: Warm low-angle sun (gold/orange tint)

    * Fill/Ambient: Dark brown bounce light for rich shadows

    * Shadows: Hard-edged or toon-filtered preferred

🌄 Skybox / Sky

    * Theme: Blood-red dusk

    * Style: Static gradient or flat-mesh dome

    * Tone: Emotional, mythic, otherworldly — not realistic
